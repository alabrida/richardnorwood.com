## Usage Rules

- Public-facing doctrine should prefer directional language unless a claim has a dated source.
- Internal planning numbers may remain as assumptions only when they are labeled as working ranges or hypotheses.
- When evidence is collected, record the source, publication date, context, and the exact sentence the claim can support.
