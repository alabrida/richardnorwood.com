# Defining the Revenue Architect Role

A Revenue Architect designs the system that growth lives inside. The role is not to add more activity for its own sake. The role is to make the business easier to understand, easier to buy from, and easier to keep aligned after the sale.

## What the Role Owns

- stage design and exit criteria
- governance and shared definitions
- tool and workflow design
- handoffs between marketing, sales, delivery, and retention
- visibility through Information Fusion and stronger operational discipline

## Relationship to the Guide

Richard's personal brand leads with the Guide role because most clients need translation before they need heavy architecture language. The architecture discipline is still underneath the work; it is simply explained in more approachable terms.

## Practical Outcome

A strong Revenue Architect produces better decisions, fewer blind spots, cleaner conversion, and stronger retention because the system stops depending on heroics to stay coherent.
