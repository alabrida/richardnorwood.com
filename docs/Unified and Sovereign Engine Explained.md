# Unified Commercial Engine and Sovereign Engine Explained

These terms are related, but they are not interchangeable.

## Unified Commercial Engine

The **Unified Commercial Engine (UCE)** is the operating model. It aligns stages, ownership, proof, handoffs, and customer follow-through across the entire commercial journey.

## Sovereign Engine

The **Sovereign Engine** is the more owned technical estate that can host or enforce that operating model when a business needs greater control over data, workflows, and infrastructure.

## Where the Managed Nervous System Fits

The **Managed Nervous System (MNS)** sits between the two. It creates visibility and automation with the current stack so the business can become more orchestrated before deciding whether a deeper rebuild is necessary.

## Practical Relationship

A business can improve its Unified Commercial Engine before it owns everything. The Sovereign Engine is the deeper build state, not the starting requirement.
