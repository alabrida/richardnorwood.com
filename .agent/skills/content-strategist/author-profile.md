Ever wondered why leads vanish or revenue doesn't match expectations? It often comes down to hidden friction points in your customer journey. As a certified Project Management Professional (PMP), I bring the delivery discipline to uncover these revenue leaks, fix the system, and turn strategy into measurable outcomes.
